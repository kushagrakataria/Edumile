About Us

Edu-miles connects underprivileged students with teachers and university students who are willing to volunteer their time to teach them. The website provides a variety of features to make it easy for teachers and students to connect and coordinate, as well as a library of educational resources.

The Problem

The Problem

Millions of children around the world do not have access to education. This is due to a variety of factors, such as poverty, lack of resources, and discrimination. Underprivileged people often have difficulty getting the education they need to succeed.

Lack of Coordination:

There is often a lack of coordination and resources to connect volunteer educators with underserved communities, making it challenging for those willing to teach to reach these areas.

Educational Inequality:

Many underprivileged and remote communities lack access to quality education, leading to a significant educational divide between urban and rural areas.

Teacher Shortages:

Remote areas frequently suffer from a shortage of qualified teachers, making it difficult to provide students with effective and consistent instruction.

Limited Connectivity:

Remote areas may have limited or no access to the internet or digital resources, restricting opportunities for online learning and access to educational materials.

Limited Educational Resources:

In remote areas, there is often a lack of educational materials, trained teachers, and adequate infrastructure, hindering the learning process.

Financial Barriers:

Underprivileged students often face financial barriers to accessing education, including the cost of school supplies, transportation, and other educational expenses.

The concept

The concept

The concept is to create a digital platform that acts as a bridge between volunteer educators, primarily teachers and university students, and underprivileged or remote communities that lack access to quality education. The platform facilitates the coordination of educational efforts, making it easier for educators to volunteer their time and expertise to teach in underserved areas.

Teacher Registration:

Teachers and university students interested in volunteering create profiles on the platform, highlighting their expertise and availability.

Area Registration:

our team registers remote areas or villages on behalf of the local community, providing essential information about location, education needs, and the number of students.

Matching Algorithm:

The platform uses an algorithm to match registered teachers with remote areas based on subjects, availability, and location proximity.

Resource Library:

The platform provides a library of educational resources for teachers to access, helping them prepare for lessons in areas with limited educational materials.

Lesson Scheduling:

Teachers can view available time slots in registered areas and schedule teaching sessions accordingly.

Community Forums:

A forum or discussion board encourages teachers, volunteers, and area representatives to share experiences, ask questions, and collaborate.

Donation Page:

Allow users, including teachers, volunteers, and supporters, to make monetary donations through the platform.
